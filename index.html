We Are Forms

is a creative design consultancy for contemporary living, established by industrial designers Carlos Tur and Elena Martínez in Valencia

Our design process is carried out from a narrative starting point, relying on historical knowledge of design and the search of new technologies and manufacturing processes, challenging industry and arquetipe standards.

We explore functionality around human habits, with a forecasting eye on ever evolving usages and ways of expression with preoccupation with simplicity, improvement and the pragmatic application of creativity; combined with a rigorous and knowledgable approach to the technical aspects of materials and manufacturing leads to results that are, at the same time, useful, engaging and relevant.
